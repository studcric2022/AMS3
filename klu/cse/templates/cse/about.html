Automobile Management System

An Automobile Management System (AMS) is a software application that helps businesses manage their automobile inventory, sales, and service. It can be used by car dealerships, rental car companies, fleet management companies, and other businesses that own or operate a fleet of vehicles.



An AMS typically includes the following features:

  • Inventory management: This allows businesses to track their inventory of vehicles, including the make, model, year, and mileage.
  • Sales management: This allows businesses to track their sales of vehicles, including the date of sale, the sales price, and the customer information.
  • Service management: This allows businesses to track their service records for vehicles, including the date of service, the service performed, and the cost of service.
  • Reporting: This allows businesses to generate reports on their automobile inventory, sales, and service.

An AMS can help businesses to improve their efficiency and profitability by automating many of the tasks involved in managing a fleet of vehicles. It can also help businesses to improve their customer service by providing them with easy access to information about their vehicles.
